## Biography

Salvatore Sanfilippo (antirez) created Redis in 2009. He led its development for over a decade before stepping back from day-to-day maintenance.

## Daily Work

- Working on personal projects
- Contributing to Redis occasionally
- Writing about software development
- Exploring new ideas

## Challenges

> "Redis was designed to be simple and fast. Maintaining that simplicity while adding features is always a tension."

## Impact

Redis is the most popular in-memory data store, powering caching and real-time features for millions of applications.
