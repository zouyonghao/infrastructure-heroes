## Biography

Brendan Gregg is a performance engineer at Intel, known for his work on DTrace, BPF, and system performance analysis. He's the author of "Systems Performance."

## Daily Work

- Performance engineering at Intel
- Developing BPF-based tools
- Writing about system performance
- Speaking at conferences

## Challenges

## Impact

Brendan's flame graphs and BPF tools have revolutionized how the industry debugs performance issues.
