## Biography

Brian Kernighan co-authored "The C Programming Language" and many influential computing books. He created AWK with Aho and Weinberger.

## Daily Work

- Teaching at Princeton University
- Writing about computing
- Mentoring students
- Giving talks

## Challenges

> "Good writing about programming is as important as good programming. People need to understand ideas clearly."

## Impact

Brian's books have taught programming to millions. AWK remains a crucial Unix tool after nearly 50 years.
