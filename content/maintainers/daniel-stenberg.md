## Biography

Daniel Stenberg created curl in 1998 and has maintained it ever since. He has been the driving force behind this ubiquitous data transfer tool for over 25 years.

## Daily Work

- Reviewing and merging contributions
- Fixing bugs and security issues
- Writing documentation and blog posts
- Managing releases and security advisories

## Challenges

> "curl is installed on every device imaginable. When there's a bug, it affects billions of installations. The responsibility is immense."

## Impact

curl is installed on over 10 billion devices, making it one of the most widely deployed software packages in history. Daniel's work directly impacts how the internet transfers data.
