## Biography

Dennis Ritchie (1941-2011) co-created Unix and designed the C programming language at Bell Labs. His work forms the foundation of modern computing.

## Legacy

- Created the C programming language
- Co-developed Unix operating system
- Turing Award winner (1983)
- National Medal of Technology recipient

## Challenges

> "Unix is very simple, it just needs a genius to understand its simplicity."

## Impact

C remains one of the most important programming languages, powering operating systems, embedded systems, and systems software worldwide.
