## Biography

Greg Kroah-Hartman is a major contributor to the Linux kernel, maintaining the stable branch and leading driver core development. He works for the Linux Foundation.

## Daily Work

- Maintaining stable kernel releases
- Reviewing driver core patches
- Mentoring new kernel developers
- Speaking at conferences about kernel development

## Challenges

> "The kernel is the foundation of the modern world's digital infrastructure. Keeping it stable while incorporating new features is a constant balancing act."

## Impact

Greg's stable kernel maintenance ensures that critical security patches reach the billions of devices running Linux, from data centers to smartphones.
