## Biography

Ken Thompson co-created Unix at Bell Labs, designed the B programming language, and co-created Go and UTF-8. He's a Turing Award winner.

## Daily Work

- Retired from Google
- Occasional contributions to computing
- Advising and mentoring
- Personal projects

## Challenges

## Impact

Ken's work on Unix fundamentally shaped computing. His contributions span five decades and continue to influence modern systems.
