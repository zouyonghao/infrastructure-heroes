## Biography

Mark Adler co-created zlib with Jean-loup Gailly. He also contributed to gzip and works at JPL on space mission software.

## Daily Work

- Maintaining zlib
- Working at NASA JPL
- Responding to security issues
- Ensuring compatibility

## Challenges

> "zlib is everywhere - in every device, every application. When there's a bug, it affects the entire world's software."

## Impact

zlib compression is used by virtually every computer and device, making Mark's maintenance critical to global software infrastructure.
