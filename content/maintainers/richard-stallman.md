## Biography

Richard Stallman founded the GNU Project in 1983 and the Free Software Foundation. He created GCC, Emacs, and the GPL license.

## Daily Work

- Advocating for free software
- Speaking worldwide
- Writing about software freedom
- Leading the GNU Project

## Challenges

> "Free software is a matter of liberty, not price. To understand the concept, you should think of 'free' as in 'free speech.'"

## Impact

The GNU Project and GPL license shaped how we think about software freedom and enabled Linux to exist.
