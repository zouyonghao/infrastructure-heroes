## Biography

Rob Pike co-created Go at Google with Ken Thompson and Robert Griesemer. He previously worked at Bell Labs on Unix, Plan 9, and co-created UTF-8.

## Daily Work

- Contributing to Go development
- Advising on language design
- Writing about programming
- Speaking at conferences

## Challenges

> "Go was designed to be simple. Resisting the urge to add features is harder than adding them."

## Impact

Go has become the language of cloud infrastructure, powering Docker, Kubernetes, and countless services at Google and beyond.
