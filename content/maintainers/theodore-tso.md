## Biography

Theodore Ts'o (Ted) is a founding member of the Linux Foundation and a prominent kernel maintainer. He is best known for creating and maintaining the ext4 filesystem, the default filesystem for most Linux distributions.

## Key Contributions

- Created and maintains ext4 filesystem
- Kernel cryptography subsystem
- Founding member of the Linux Foundation
- Former CTO of the Linux Foundation

## Impact

Ted's work on ext4 ensures reliable storage for billions of Linux systems worldwide. His leadership in the Linux Foundation has helped shape the governance and sustainability of the entire open source ecosystem.
