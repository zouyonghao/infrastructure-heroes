## Biography

Thomas Gleixner (tglx) is one of the most senior Linux kernel maintainers, responsible for core subsystems including timers, IRQs, and x86 architecture support. He has been involved with kernel development since the early days and is widely respected for his deep technical expertise.

## Key Contributions

- Timer subsystem maintenance
- IRQ (Interrupt Request) handling
- x86 architecture support
- Real-time Linux (PREEMPT_RT) development

## Impact

Thomas's work on core kernel infrastructure affects every Linux system in the world. His deep understanding of low-level hardware interaction and scheduling makes him a critical contributor to the kernel's stability and performance.
