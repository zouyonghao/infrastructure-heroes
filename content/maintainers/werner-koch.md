## Biography

Werner Koch created GnuPG in 1997 and has maintained it since. He is the guardian of open-source encryption that protects communications worldwide.

## Daily Work

- Maintaining GnuPG and related cryptographic libraries
- Responding to security issues
- Working on cryptographic standards
- Coordinating with the privacy community

## Challenges

> "Privacy is a fundamental right. GnuPG helps protect it, but maintaining critical security software with limited resources is challenging."

## Impact

GnuPG secures email encryption and software package signing for Linux distributions. Werner's work protects the privacy and security of millions.
