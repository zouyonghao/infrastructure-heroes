## Biography

Yukihiro "Matz" Matsumoto created Ruby in 1995 with a focus on programmer happiness. He continues to guide the language's development from Japan.

## Daily Work

- Guiding Ruby language design
- Reviewing proposals and features
- Speaking at Ruby conferences worldwide
- Collaborating with the Ruby core team

## Challenges

> "I designed Ruby to make programmers happy. Balancing that goal with performance and practical concerns is an ongoing challenge."

## Impact

Ruby and Ruby on Rails revolutionized web development, inspiring countless frameworks and proving that developer experience matters.
