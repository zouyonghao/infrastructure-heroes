### Overview

HashiCorp Consul is a service networking solution to automate network configurations, discover services, and enable secure connectivity.

### Importance

- Powers service discovery
- Foundation for service mesh
- Critical for distributed systems
- Used by enterprises worldwide

### Key Features

- Service discovery
- Health checking
- Key/value store
- Service mesh

### Sustainability

Consul is backed by HashiCorp with strong commercial support. The open-source edition remains actively developed.
