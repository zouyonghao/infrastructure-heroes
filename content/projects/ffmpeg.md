### Overview

FFmpeg is a complete, cross-platform solution to record, convert, and stream audio and video. It is used by virtually every video application.

### Importance

- Powers video processing everywhere
- Foundation of streaming services
- Critical for media applications
- Used by YouTube, VLC, and more

### Key Features

- Codec support (hundreds)
- Transcoding
- Streaming
- Filtering

### Sustainability

FFmpeg is developed by volunteers with limited funding. Despite its critical role in video infrastructure, the project operates with minimal resources.
