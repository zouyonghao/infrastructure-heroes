### Overview

HAProxy is a free, very fast, and reliable reverse proxy offering high availability, load balancing, and proxying for TCP and HTTP-based applications.

### Importance

- Powers high-traffic websites
- Foundation for load balancing
- Critical for application availability
- Used by GitHub, Reddit, and more

### Key Features

- Layer 4 and 7 load balancing
- SSL termination
- Health checking
- Rate limiting

### Sustainability

HAProxy has commercial backing from HAProxy Technologies. The open-source project benefits from corporate support.
