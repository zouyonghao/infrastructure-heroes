### Overview

Kubernetes is an open-source container orchestration system for automating deployment, scaling, and management of containerized applications.

### Importance

- Industry standard for container orchestration
- Powers cloud infrastructure worldwide
- Foundation of cloud-native computing
- Used by most major enterprises

### Key Features

- Automatic scaling and load balancing
- Service discovery and networking
- Self-healing capabilities
- Rolling updates and rollbacks

### Sustainability

Kubernetes is supported by the Cloud Native Computing Foundation (CNCF) with strong corporate backing from Google, Red Hat, Microsoft, and many others.
