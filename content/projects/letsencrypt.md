### Overview

Let's Encrypt is a nonprofit certificate authority providing free TLS certificates to more than 300 million websites, making HTTPS accessible to everyone.

### Importance

- Powers encryption for millions of websites
- Democratized HTTPS adoption
- Foundation for web security
- Critical for privacy online

### Key Features

- Free certificates
- Automated issuance (ACME)
- 90-day certificates
- Wildcard support

### Sustainability

Let's Encrypt is run by ISRG with strong corporate sponsors including Mozilla, Cisco, Chrome, and major tech companies.
