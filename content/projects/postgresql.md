### Overview

PostgreSQL is a powerful, open source object-relational database system with over 35 years of active development. It has earned a strong reputation for reliability, feature robustness, and performance.

### Importance

- One of the most popular databases globally
- Used by Apple, Spotify, Instagram, Reddit
- Foundation of many SaaS products
- Strong presence in enterprise environments

### Key Features

- ACID compliance
- Complex queries and indexing
- Full-text search
- JSON/JSONB support
- Extension ecosystem
- Multi-version concurrency control (MVCC)
